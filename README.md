# What This Project Is

This project is a learning platform designed to guide learners step by step as they build new skills while providing feedback necessary to reflect and improve. It has advanced progress tracking capabilities, which allow learners to see their progress clearly. It is also way for teachers and experts to support education, by creating structured learning paths.

# Who Is This Project For

This project is for learners who struggle with or want to improve their learning quality, efficiency and satisfaction. It is also for teachers and experts who want to improve education level by sharing their knowledge in a structured way. Learnes will be able to use their knowledge in real-world scenarios and receive meaningful feedback. Teachers and experts will be able to create learning paths with the help of AI.

# Problems And How This Project Solves Them

This project's goal is to **improve learning quality, efficiency and satisfaction** by tackling the most common problems learners face when learning.

![diagram summarizing problems and solutions](./docs/res/diagrams/problems-and-solutions.excalidraw.png)

## Not Knowing Where To Start

When a learner wants to study something new, the abundance of books, videos, websites, and articles can feel overwhelming. They often don’t know where to start or in what order to approach the material, and that uncertainty can quickly sap motivation. A mentor could provide guidance, but not everyone has access to one—or can afford it.

Our solution is structured learning paths that act like a mentor. They guide learners step by step, in the right order, providing not only the knowledge but also ways to test understanding through quizzes and opportunities to apply it in exercises and projects. This approach helps learners build real skills and confidence, rather than just acquiring theory.

## Not Knowing How To Apply The Knowledge

Imagine a learner who already has a path to follow. They’re studying the theory, watching video summaries, and even asking questions to an AI chatbot. But there’s still one big problem: they can’t actually use what they’ve learned in the real world, because all of it has stayed in theory. To really understand and remember something, you have to use it. Theory helps you see how things work, but it’s not enough to learn a skill.

Our answer is simple: quizzes, exercises, and projects. They help test what’s been learned, put it into practice, and build something real with it. That way, the learner doesn’t just know the material—they understand it deeply and know how to use it when it matters.

## Lack Of Progress Overview

Even with a clear learning path and opportunities to practice knowledge, learning can be made even more effective. A learner shouldn’t just follow the path blindly—they need to reflect on their progress, identify skills they struggle with, and adapt to new challenges. Without being able to see progress, learning can feel frustrating, and the sense of joy and satisfaction can quickly fade.

Our solution is advanced progress tracking. It shows what the learner has already learned, where they are now, and what comes next. It provides meaningful feedback, highlighting areas that need improvement. Progress tracking also measures psychological and pedagogical traits, giving insight into the learner’s strengths, habits, and tendencies, which helps personalize the learning process. This makes it much more than a simple progress bar—it’s a tool for guided growth and continuous improvement
