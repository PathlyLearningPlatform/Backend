# Structured Learning

Structured learning will increase motivation and perseverance by fostering **action orientation** and providing **necessary theory**, **practical exercises**, **quizes** and **projects**. Learning process will heavily rely on practical exercises, which will allow learners to apply theory in practise as quickly as possible. Theory is also important, learning paths will provide necessary concepts and quizes that will test their understanding, but not to many to avoid overwhelm. Learning paths will also contain real-world projects, that will allow learners to apply everything they have learned in practice. In summary learning paths will provide **structured learning experience** that focuses on **learning by doing**.

[more on learning paths feature](./features.md#learning-paths)

# Projects, Exercises And Feedback

**Projects** and **exercises** will build **confidence** by applying knowledge in real world scenarios and prove that learner is **competent** and is heading in the **right direction**. Leraners will receive **feedback**, that will let them know if exercises and projects meet requirements and give them hints about possible improvements. We will foster **growth mindset**, which will allow learners to **reflect** on their work and **improve**.

[more on projects, exercises and feedback](./features.md#learning-paths)

# Progress Assessment

Progress assessment is necessary in improving learning quality and giving learners information **what they have learned**, **where they are now**, **what is next**.
Our measurements will include progress overview and **mastery behaviors (persistence, effort, resilience and challenge seeking)** as well as useful statistics and insights.
